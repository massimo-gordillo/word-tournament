Privacy Policy for Wordle Tournament Tracker

Effective date: March 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Wordle Tournament Tracker ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and data required to run core app features:

  • Account information: email address and authentication data managed through Supabase Auth.
  • Profile information: display name.
  • App content you submit: Wordle submission text, calculated score, submission date/time.
  • Tournament data: tournaments you create or join, participation status, scores, and leaderboard-related records.
  • Technical/session data: basic service logs generated by our backend provider to operate and secure the service.

We do not use your data for advertising personalization.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account and sign-in sessions.
  • Process daily submissions and tournament scoring.
  • Show tournament standings, results, and participation status.
  • Enforce app rules and limits (for example, tournament and submission constraints).
  • Maintain app security, reliability, and abuse prevention.

3. Legal Basis (Where Applicable)

If you are in a region requiring legal bases for data processing (such as the EEA/UK), we process data based on:

  • Performance of a contract (providing the app service you request).
  • Legitimate interests (service security, fraud prevention, reliability).
  • Compliance with legal obligations where required.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We share data only as needed to provide the service:

  • Service provider: Supabase (authentication, database, and hosting infrastructure).
  • Legal/compliance: if required by law or valid legal process.

5. Data Retention

We retain account and gameplay data for as long as your account remains active or as needed to provide tournament history and app functionality.

If you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize personal data where reasonably possible, subject to legal, security, and legitimate operational requirements.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including authenticated access controls and row-level security policies in our database. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than your own, depending on infrastructure used by our service providers. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards under applicable law.

9. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data.

To submit a privacy request, use the contact method in Section 10.

10. Contact Us

For privacy questions or requests, contact us through the GitHub repository where this policy is published (for example, by opening an issue and marking it as a privacy request).

If you prefer, replace this section with a dedicated support email before publishing to Google Play.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version at the same URL and revise the Effective date above.
